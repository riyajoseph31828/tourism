    • Malappuram


    Malappuram is a city in the Indian state of Kerala, spread over an area of 58.20 km².
    The first municipality in the district formed in 1970, Malappuram serves as the administrative
    headquarters of Malappuram district. Divided into 40 electoral wards, the city has
    a population density of 1,742 per square kilometre.
