    • Kollam


    Kollam is a city in the state of Kerala, on India's Malabar Coast. It’s known as a trade hub and for its beaches,
    like lively Kollam and secluded Thirumullavaram. Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Police Museum has artifacts tracing
    the history of the police force. Nearby, Ashtamudi Lake is a gateway to the Kerala backwaters, a network of
    waterways rich with vegetation. The striped 1902 Tangasseri Lighthouse has ocean views.
    Area: 73.03 km²
    Elevation: 3 m
    Weather: 27 °C, Wind SE at 8 km/h, 85% Humidity
